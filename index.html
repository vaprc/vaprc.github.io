About

The Virginia Precision Rifle Club was formed to promote precision rifle shooting as a sport in Virginia. We intend to hold monthly club matches, promote the sport, encourage responsible firearms ownership, and help new shooters get started.

Matches

We intend to hold monthly club matches, promote the sport, encourage responsible firearms ownership, and help new shooters get started. We are an affiliate of the Southeastern Precision Rifle Club Association , match results are submitted to SEPRCA and count towards their yearly standings.
