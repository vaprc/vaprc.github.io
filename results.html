Results/Standings

Congratulations to Brian Sutphin, winner of the January 2017 match!

Match results are calculated and posted here. Overall scores are calculated by taking the shooter's monthly match score, dividing it by the monthly winners score, and multiplying that number by 100. If one shooter scores 30 points, and first place scored 40 points then the shooter would receive 75 points for the yearly points race (30/40=0.75, 0.75*100=75 points).

We are a member of the PRS Club Series, match results are submitted to PRS and count towards their yearly standings. Please make sure that your name is the same as used in your PRS standings so that points earned at VPRC contribute to PRS Club Series results.
